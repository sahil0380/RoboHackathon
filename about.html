ROBO HACKATHON

Robo Hackathon is a project dedicated to building a functional and cost-effective robot without sacrificing efficiency or innovation. The goal is to create a smart, adaptable robotic system using affordable components and optimized design principles. By focusing on frugality, the project aims to make robotics accessible to students, hobbyists, and developers with a passion for technology.

The project emphasizes creative problem-solving, leveraging open-source software, 3D-printed parts, and repurposed electronics to keep costs low. It explores automation, AI integration, and mechanical efficiency, ensuring that even with limited resources, the robot remains capable of performing complex tasks.
